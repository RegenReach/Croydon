Who We Are

A Neighbourhood Bakery.
Built on Quality.

Croydon Bake House has been serving Melbourne with freshly baked goods from our kitchen on Main Street. What started as a beloved local bakery has grown into a trusted wholesale supplier for cafes, restaurants, and businesses all across Melbourne.

Every pie, slice, scroll and quiche is made in-house using quality ingredients. No shortcuts, no compromise. Our loyal customers keep coming back because they can taste the difference.
